# Phrasebank Playbook

Use after the main argument and section role are clear.

Evidence strength:
- Strong: show, demonstrate, establish, reveal, identify.
- Moderate: suggest, indicate, support the view that, are consistent with, point to.
- Speculative: may reflect, could arise from, appears to, seems likely.

Use precise gap language: remains poorly understood, has received limited attention, few studies have addressed.

Use limitation language tied to a real source of uncertainty.

Implications must stay within the evidence boundary.
