# Style Guardrails

Use for mechanical and stylistic checks after the main rewrite.

- Prefer cautious, precise prose.
- Avoid contractions and rhetorical questions.
- Define abbreviations on first use.
- Use numerals for measurements and a space before units.
- Keep notation consistent.
- Flag and soften prove, conclusively, unprecedented, best, superior, and unqualified first.
- Do not invent references or change quantitative values.
